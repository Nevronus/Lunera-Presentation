The Silent Epidemic: Preventable Blindness in South Asia

  • Millions in South Asia suffer from avoidable blindness due to Diabetic Retinopathy (DR), Glaucoma, and Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD).
  • Critical barriers include lack of access to affordable, timely screening and a shortage of specialist care.
  • Example: Bangladesh – 14 million people with diabetes; 1 in 3 are at risk of DR.
